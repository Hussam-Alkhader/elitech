Privacy Policy for Elitech Co.

If we make such a transfer, we will put appropriate measures in place to ensure that your information is adequately protected at all times.

Data Controller and Data Processor

We process two main types of personal data:

  • Customer Data – Personal data provided by our customers and their end-users for processing.

Elitech Co. is the processor of Customer Data and the Controller of Other Data.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

Elitech Co. collects, processes, and retains information about you when you visit our website if you choose to provide it. You may choose to provide some of this information directly, such as when you submit the “Contact Us” form or “Request Demo” form. Elitech Co. uses this information to measure site usage, improve content, and ensure safety and security.

You can make choices about our collection and use of your data. You may decline when you are asked to provide personal data. The data we collect depends on the context of your interactions with Elitech Co. and your choices, including your privacy settings.
